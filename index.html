Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do you teach adults?

    We teach anyone. It doesn’t matter if you’re 5 years old, or 105 years old. We are standing by, ready to help.
  • What is the cost for lessons?

    Weekly 30 minute lessons are available for a $119 monthly tuition. 60 minute lessons cost $238 per month.
  • Is the studio reserved just for students?

    Absolutely not. The recording studio is open to the public. Give us a call to duscuss your next project!
  • Can I work at Launch Pad?

    We love reading resumes! You can send yours to contact@launchpadindy.com.
  • Do you sell instruments?

    We are not a music store. Our focus is entirely on instruction and recording. We have great relationships with local stores and we are more than happy to help you get connected to amazing gear and quality retail options.
  • What is Rock Band?

    Rock band is our unique program, designed to give kids/youth the full band experience. They rehearse weekly, perform regularly, and record together in our studio. Learn more HERE
  • How much does it cost to record?

    We have made professional recording affordable on any budget. Our studio rate is just $60 per hour. And the best part...we DO NOT charge additional fees for the engineer or for mixing and mastering by the song. You simply pay for the time.
  • What instruments / disciplines do you teach?

    We teach guitar, bass guitar, voice, drums, piano/keyboard, audio engineering, and electronic music production.
  • Do you offer sibling discounts?

    We do not. Our rates are so low, and our instructors work independently of one another. This makes it impossible for us to discount our lesson offerings.
  • Is this a safe place for my kids?

    Absolutely, yes. There is nothing we take more seriously. All of our instructors are drug-free, and must pass a background check. And that's just the beginning. We seek out and recruit only staff members who are positive and encouraging people. If they don't fit that mold, we find someone else. In addition, we record video in every lesson room and display it live in our waiting area.
