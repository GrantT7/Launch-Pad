OUR PROGRAMS

MUSIC LESSONS

Here at the Launch Pad, we offer music lessons for all ages and skill levels.


The music lesson program at The Launch Pad is truly a cut above the standard music store experience. Everything is designed with performance in mind!


Students meet with one of our talented, dedicated instructors for 30 minutes each week, and right away we have them learning the music that inspires them. Our entire program and facility are designed to take learning music into a new dimension, that’s as collaborative as it is creative.


Once our youth students get the basics, it’s onward to our Rock Band program, where they have opportunities to rehearse, perform and record music with their peers and with the pros. The “lessons” they learn along the way? …teamwork, accountability, confidence and self-expression.

ABOUT US

OUR STORY

The West Side finally has a creative and collaborative space for learning and creating music. Located in Downtown Plainfield, The Launch Pad has become a destination for artists in Avon, Brownsburg, Danville, Mooresville, Camby, Monrovia, Clayton, Wayne Township and all of Greater Indianapolis. We offer private music lessons on guitar, bass guitar, drums, piano, voice and recording arts. We are the only place you can find “Rock Band”, and we have one of the best recording studios in Indianapolis. Whether you want to learn the basics, take your talent to the next level, or hit the recording studio, contact us today and find your voice!


We have top-notch instructors and a killer space for artists. The Launch Pad features 5 unique music lesson rooms, a full-service recording studio, and an open rehearsal space for group lessons, clinics, and our Rock Band rehearsals. We can even host your next birthday party or come to your location for clinics and recording sessions.


At The Launch Pad, we believe that when you put an instrument in a kid’s hand AND you show them to the stage, you give them the confidence to succeed. We are equipping and encouraging artists to find their voice…in music and in life!

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • What is the cost for lessons?

    Weekly 30 minute lessons are available for a $119 monthly tuition. 60 minute lessons cost $238 per month.
  • Do you offer Week-To-Week pricing?

    We do not, this ensures that students have a consistent lesson time each month.
  • Do you offer online lessons?

    Yes, we are equipped to do online lessons via Zoom, which can be set up with your instructor.
  • Is my student too young?

    Every student is different and young students will have varying levels of success. We typically recommend starting lessons once they enter elementary school, but we can always do a trial lesson to see if they are ready even sooner!
  • Do you teach adults?

    Absolutely! It's never too late to get started on a new hobby or to finally discover a passion for music.
  • Do you sell instruments?

    We are not a music store. Our focus is entirely on instruction and getting students ready to play live! We have great relationships with local stores and we are more than happy to help you get connected to amazing gear and quality retail options.
  • What is Rock Band?

    Rock band is our unique program, designed to give kids/youth the full band experience. They rehearse weekly and perform regularly.
  • What instruments / disciplines do you teach?

    We teach guitar, bass guitar, voice, drums, piano/keyboard, audio engineering, and electronic music production.
  • Does The Launch Pad provide instruments or should I buy one?

    Students are encouraged to purchase their own instrument. However we can let students try out an instrument before you buy one. Drum sets and guitar amps are provided in lessons.
  • Is this a safe place for my kids?

    Absolutely, yes. There is nothing we take more seriously. All of our instructors are drug-free, and must pass a background check. And that's just the beginning. We seek out and recruit only staff members who are positive and encouraging people. If they don't fit that mold, we find someone else. In addition, we record video in every lesson room and display it live in our waiting area.
  • Can I work at Launch Pad?

    We love reading resumes! You can send yours to contact@launchpadindy.com.

    Yes, you can read it by clicking HERE.
