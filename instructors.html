Check out our Instructors!

Ryan Freeman

Ryan is our owner and a multi-talented instructor and recording engineer. Whether it’s drum lessons, bass guitar lessons or recording lessons, his students all agree…they love their teacher! He is also a mentor in our Rock Band program. He began playing drums and bass at 9 years old under the careful instruction of his dad (a killer musician and a great producer). He graduated from SAE Institute in Nashville, TN with a degree in recording arts, and worked on Music Row before returning to Indianapolis to start The Launch Pad. He has shared the stage with well known worship leaders, blues musicians, and a laundry list of local indie and rock groups. As an instructor, he reaches students by focusing on their own goals and offering consistent encouragement. Whether you are the next big drummer, bassist or recording engineer, you have found the right guy to take you there!

Grant Tyler

Grant brings the best of both worlds to our program. He’s a classically-trained pianist thats been playing for years, yet also thrives in modern day pop keys as well as improvisation in a group setting. He also plays a mean guitar and teaches electronic music production (beat-making, dub step, etc…). The Indiana University School of Music has given him all of the right tools he needs to help you reach your goals. He has been performing for more than a decade with groups ranging from concert bands, live pep bands, and even the Hendricks County Symphony Orchestra. Last but not least, Grant is a seasoned producer, with years of experience in genres including rock, pop, hip-hop, and electronic music. Whether you want traditional piano lessons (minus the grumpy old lady) or you are ready to play your favorite pop and rock songs, Grant is the right instructor for you.
